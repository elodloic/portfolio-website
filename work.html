Meet App

A progressive web app built with React that allows users to search for and view information about upcoming events in their area. Event data is fetched from the Google Calendar API, after OAuth login with a Google account. The app features a responsive design, charts generated based on the loaded data (recharts), offline capabilities and local installation.
